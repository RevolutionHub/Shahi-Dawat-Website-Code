ABOUT US

Welcome to Shahi Dawat – A Royal Feast of Indian Flavors


Since 2011, Shahi Dawat has been serving authentic Indian cuisine with a rich blend of traditional spices and flavors. Known for our legendary Butter Chicken and mouthwatering Chicken Tikka Masala, we bring you the true taste of India in every bite.

At Shahi Dawat, we believe in offering a royal dining experience, where every dish is crafted with passion and perfection. Whether you're craving aromatic curries, sizzling tandoori delights, or freshly baked naan, our menu is designed to satisfy your cravings.

Join us for an unforgettable culinary journey and experience the essence of Indian hospitality. Indulge in the finest flavors, only at Shahi Dawat – where every meal is a celebration!
