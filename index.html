about us

Kaki Ka Kitchen

The Indian Street Flavours - Where Indian Flavours meet kiwi streets!

At Kaki Ka Kitchen, we bring the vibrant and authentic flavours of Indian street food right to your doorstep. Our food truck is all about serving traditional, mouthwatering dishes made with the freshest ingredients and spices that capture the essence of India's rich culinary heritage. Whether you're craving the crispy Vadapav or a refreshing Mango Lassi, every bite promises an explosion of flavour, crafted with love and care.

  • Authentic Indian street food made with traditional recipes.
  • Fresh, high-quality ingredients in every dish.
  • A memorable culinary experience, right here in Auckland.
